Axe Preferences


Like all good apps Axe has some preferences.


Preferences


1. Status of Axe.

2. Enabling this will launch Axe, but not enable it, when you login.

3. Enabling this will enable Axe as soon as it opens. Enable #2 and #3 if you want to automatically enable Axe as soon as you login to your computer.

4. Checks for updates

5. Opens the help book, you probably used that to get to here!
